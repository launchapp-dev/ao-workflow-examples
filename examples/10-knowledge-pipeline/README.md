# Knowledge & Decision Pipeline

A multi-stage pipeline that turns raw knowledge into strategic questions, actionable items, and founder-ready decision packages.

## The Pipeline

```
Knowledge Base (products, architecture, competitive, repos)
    ↓
Question Generator (daily)
    ↓ generates 3-5 strategic questions
    ↓ "What assumptions are we making that might be wrong?"
    ↓ "What risks are we not tracking?"
    ↓
[Human answers questions]
    ↓
Action Extractor
    ↓ turns answered questions into concrete action items
    ↓ with owners, priorities, effort estimates, dependencies
    ↓
Decision Packager (weekly)
    ↓ batches actions into founder-ready decision packages
    ↓ "approve / reject / defer" format
    ↓
[Founder reviews in 5 minutes]
    ↓
Decision Processor
    ↓ updates action statuses based on founder decisions
    ↓
Cross-Repo Executor
    ↓ executes approved actions in target repos
```

## What Makes This Special

- **Questions are specific, not generic** — grounded in actual knowledge base content
- **Actions are concrete** — specific repos, specific changes, specific acceptance criteria
- **Decision packages are scannable** — a founder can review 10 decisions in 5 minutes
- **Execution is automated** — approved actions become real PRs in target repos

## Key Insight

The gap between "we should do X" and "X is done" is where most organizations lose momentum. This pipeline closes that gap by making decisions easy and execution automatic.
